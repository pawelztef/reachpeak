Logo Design

Need a logo design for your business? Reachpeak can help you create a logo that portrays the image of your company. We can then incorporate that logo into your new web site design. We create the logos at print quality resolution, then create several, low resolution, web versions of your logo.

Graphic Design for Print

Whether you require a catalog or a 1 color business card, we work with you to design a piece that meets your needs and exceeds your expectations. From custom print design and photography to copywriting and production, Reachpeak can manage your project from conception to delivery.

Infographic Design

Infographics is a great way to illustrate some facts and point people to a product or service. The data in the infographic shows that you know the topic well. Information is displayed in a easy to digest way. Our experienced illustrators can help you create and distribute your infographic. Obtaining proper links from other infographic websites helps your website rank better on search engines and sets your authority in your industry.

Visual Identity

Your visual brand identity is how you stand apart from your competitors. It influences how consumers perceive your brand. At Reachpeak, we help you identify who you are and who you want to appeal to. We’ll help you choose a color palette using color psychology; select your typography, and design a unique logo (or refresh your current one).
